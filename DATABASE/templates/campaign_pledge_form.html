The Anthology of Inquiry

The Student Research Fund at UPHSD Molino is more than a financial instrument; it is a testament to our commitment to the intellectual legacy of our institution. In an era of rapid technological and social transition, we believe that students possess the unique vantage point required to identify and solve tomorrow's challenges.

This fund provides grants for laboratory equipment, archival access, field-work travel, and publication costs. Every contribution ensures that a student’s curiosity is never silenced by a lack of resources.
