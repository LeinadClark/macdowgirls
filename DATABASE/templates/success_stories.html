Stories of Collective Change

The Jeepney Driver Subsidy

In response to the 2026 transport crisis, the UPHSD community raised over ₱150,000 to provide fuel vouchers for 50 local drivers, ensuring student commutes remained affordable.

Impact: 50 Families Supported

Campus Stray Sanctuary

Through the Animal Welfare Fund, we established three permanent feeding stations and completed a vaccination drive for all neighborhood strays.

Impact: 100% Vaccination Rate
