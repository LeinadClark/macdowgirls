Privacy Policy

Data Collection & Transparency

We collect personal information necessary for processing donations and student verifications. This includes names, contact details, academic standing for scholarship recipients, and transaction histories. We treat every piece of data with the same reverence as our physical archives.

Donor Protection

Donor identities may be kept anonymous upon request. For public recognitions, we only display the information explicitly authorized by the benefactor. Financial information is encrypted via industry-standard protocols and never stored on our local university servers.
